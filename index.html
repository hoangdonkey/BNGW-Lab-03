Features:

  • Play as a Highly Trained Monster Slayer for Hire

    • Trained from early childhood and mutated to have superhuman skills, strength, and reflexes, witchers are a socially ostracized counterbalance to the monster-infested world in which they live.

    1. Gruesomely destroy foes as a professional monster hunter armed with a range of upgradeable weapons, mutating potions, and combat magic.
    2. Hunt down a wide variety of exotic monsters, from savage beasts prowling mountain passes to cunning supernatural predators lurking in the shadowy back alleys of densely populated cities.
    3. Invest your rewards to upgrade your weaponry and buy custom armor, or spend them on horse races, card games, fist fighting and other pleasures life brings.

    1. Built for endless adventure, the massive open world of The Witcher sets new standards in terms of size, depth and complexity.
    2. Traverse a fantastical open world: explore forgotten ruins, caves and shipwrecks, trade with merchants and dwarven smiths in cities, and hunt across open plains, amidst mountains and at sea.
    3. In a time of war, track down the child of prophecy, a living weapon of great power whose coming was foretold in ancient elven legends.
    4. Make choices that go beyond good and evil and face their far-reaching consequences.
